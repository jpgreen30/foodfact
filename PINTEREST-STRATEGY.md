## Pinterest Account Setup

### Business Account
- Convert to business account (free)
- Claim website: foodfactscanner.com
- Install Pinterest Tag for analytics
- Enable rich pins (auto-update when content changes)

### Boards to Create
1. **Baby Food Safety** (main board for all safety pins)
2. **Prenatal Nutrition** (for pregnancy-related content)
3. **Healthy Baby Recipes** (recipe pins related to safe foods)
4. **Parenting Tips** (general parenting advice)
5. **Product Reviews** (brand rankings, testing results)

## Posting Schedule

**Daily (minimum):**
- 3-5 new pins per day
- Mix of new content and evergreen pins
- Post during peak times: 2-4 PM and 8-10 PM EST

**Weekly:**
- Create 5-10 new pin designs per article
- Vary images (infographics, quotes, how-to visuals)
- Update boards with fresh content

## SEO Optimization

**Pin Descriptions:**
- Include primary keyword in first 50 characters
- Use relevant hashtags (5-10 per pin)
- Mention target audience: "for parents", "new moms", "expecting mothers"
- Add call-to-action: "Learn more", "Read full guide", "Get the checklist"

**Board Titles & Descriptions:**
- Use keywords: "baby food safety", "healthy baby", "pregnancy nutrition"
- Fill all description fields completely
- Include links back to blog where appropriate

## Metrics to Track
- Monthly Pinterest viewers
- Pin saves (repins)
- Click-through rate (CTR) to blog
- Top performing pins
- Traffic from Pinterest in Google Analytics

## Tools
- **Canva** (free/paid) - create pin designs
- **Tailwind** (paid) - schedule pins automatically
- **Pinterest Business Hub** - analytics and insights
